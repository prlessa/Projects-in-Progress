Elden Ring Impressions

Elden Ring - 2022 (FromSoftware)

Elden Ring is a great new game and maybe the game of the year (no pressure). Their open world is fantastic. Multiple maps and dungeons to explore and enemies to defeat makes this game be the better choice to pass your time. The game is a collaborative project between director Hidetaka Miyazaki (Demon's Souls) and novelist George R.R. Martin (A Song of Ice and fire)
Do you want to discover more about lands between? Play Elden Ring today!

“And yes you can die. But it will return to fight again. Because that's how a champion — or a lord — is born.” - game synopsis.
